# Stakeholder Summary

## 1. Patients 

The main users of the HealthBridge Telehealth Platform are patients and they need a system that is easy to use so they can schedule their appointments, complete user's forms and attend virtual healthcare visits without any problems. Since many patients may be elderly or live outside of the city then the platform should work on a desktop as well as on a mobile device. The users feedback will help to make sure that the system is simple to use, accessible and secure especially since it will be handling personal health information.

## 2. Healthcare Providers

The healthcare providers will use the platform to manage appointments, review patient forms and meet with patients through telehealth visits. These providers need a reliable system that can help them stay organized as well as reduce any scheduling errors. So their input can influence features such as calendar integrations, a quick access to patients information and a dependable scheduling system that supports their daily work.

## 3. System Administrators 

The system administrators are responsible for managing the platform and keeping it secure. Since they need tools to create user accounts, assign roles, monitor system activity and protect patients sensitive information. Their feedback affects the requirements related to security, user management, system management and HIPAA compliance.

## 4. HealthBridge Management

The HealthBridge Management wants the platform to meet the business goals while being able to provide a positive experience for patients and healthcare providers. They need a system that is secure, reliable and able to grow as more users join the platform. Their input will help guide the requirements related to performance, compliance, accessibility and long term maintenance.
